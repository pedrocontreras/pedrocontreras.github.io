About Me

My career has a strong focus on the challenges faced by business in understanding and managing data. One of the main concerns in my work is to transform these rich data sources into information, so that it can be readily available for the decision making process. I have a strong commitment to knowledge dissemination fulfilled through the publication of my work and teaching.

Research Interests

My research interest lies on machine learning, data analysis, data modelling, distributed systems, data storage, networking technologies, data mining, information retrieval, pattern recognition and decision support systems
